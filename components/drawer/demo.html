Left Drawer

This is a left-positioned drawer. It slides in from the left side of the screen.

You can close it by:

  • Clicking the X button
  • Clicking the backdrop
  • Pressing Escape key
 Right Drawer

This drawer slides in from the right side.

 Top Drawer

This drawer slides down from the top.

 Bottom Drawer

This drawer slides up from the bottom.

 Full Width Drawer

This drawer takes the full width/height of the viewport.

 No Backdrop

This drawer has no backdrop overlay.

 No Auto Dismiss

This drawer cannot be dismissed by clicking outside or pressing Escape.

You must click the X button to close it.

 Persistent Drawer

This drawer is persistent - it has no close button and cannot be dismissed.

 Push Content

This drawer pushes the main content instead of overlaying it.

Notice how the main content shifts when this drawer opens.
