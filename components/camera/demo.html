Camera Component Demo

Access your device camera with customizable settings and capture functionality.

Live Camera Feed

Captured Photos

Features

  • Live camera feed access
  • Front/back camera switching
  • Multiple resolution options
  • Photo capture with flash effect
  • Mirror mode for selfies
  • Device selection
  • Built-in camera controls
  • Error handling

Controls

  • ▶️ Start - Start camera feed
  • ⚫ Capture - Take photo
  • 🔄 Switch - Toggle front/back camera
  • ⏹️ Stop - Stop camera feed

Resolution Options

  • QVGA - 320x240 (low quality, fast)
  • VGA - 640x480 (standard quality)
  • HD - 1280x720 (high quality)
  • Full HD - 1920x1080 (very high quality)
  • 4K - 3840x2160 (ultra quality, slow)

Browser Permissions

This component requires camera permissions. Your browser will prompt you to allow camera access.

Note: Camera access only works over HTTPS or localhost for security reasons.
