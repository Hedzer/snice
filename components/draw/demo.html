Lazy Brush

The lazy brush creates smooth, professional-looking lines by making the brush follow your cursor with a slight delay and within a configurable radius. This helps eliminate jitter and creates more organic-looking strokes.

Auto Circle

When enabled, Auto Circle creates smooth, organic shapes from your rough drawings. It works by: 1) Grouping points into time-based clusters and taking the centroid of each cluster (preserving the natural flow and wobbles from your drawing), 2) Removing sharp angles, 3) Applying Chaikin's corner-cutting algorithm for smoothing, and 4) Closing the shape with a smooth curve. This preserves the character of your drawing while creating flowing, professional-looking shapes. Adjust the closing curve points to control the smoothness of the connection.
