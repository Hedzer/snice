Kanban Board Demo

Drag and drop cards between columns. Click on a card to see details.

Features

  • Drag and drop cards between columns
  • Click on cards to view details
  • Column limits (In Progress has a limit of 3)
  • Card labels and assignees
  • Color-coded cards and columns
  • Card counts per column
